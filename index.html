As winter approached, he realized they didn't have enough warm clothing to face the bitter cold. Despite her best efforts, Asher couldn't afford the essentials after paying for food and rent. How Donations Help

Collection

We collect your gently used clothes, examine them for rips or tears, and then send them to the YMCA.

YMCA Drop-off

Your donations are delivered to our local YMCA partner where they're carefully sorted and organized.

Distribution

Your clothes find new homes with immigrant families in need, providing warmth and comfort when they need it most.

What to Donate

What We Accept

  • Gently used clothing (all sizes)
  • Winter coats, scarves, and gloves
  • Shoes in good condition
  • Blankets and bedding

Condition Guidelines

All items should be clean, free of stains, and without holes or significant wear and tear.
